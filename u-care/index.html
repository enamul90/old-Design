About Us

Welcome tos UCare

We set ourselves apart from other home care companies in a variety of ways:

  • UCare is independently owned and operated by a Registered Nurse with over 25 years of experience.
  • Our company is a reputable and respected business, accredited by the Better Business Bureau with an A+ rating.
  • As a leader in this industry, we aim to serve the community through our involvement in the Livonia and Plymouth Chambers of Commerce.
  • Unlike franchises, we utilize a small team approach, assuring patients are assigned a dependable care team that will best fit each patient’s specific needs.
